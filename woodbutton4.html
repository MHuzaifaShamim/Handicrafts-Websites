Wood Details

Wood handicrafts encompass a wide range of artistic creations crafted from wood through various techniques. Wood, being a versatile and abundant natural material, has been a popular medium for artisans to express their creativity and produce both functional and decorative items. Wood handicrafts can vary in styles, designs, and purposes, showcasing the artisan's skill and the unique characteristics of different wood types.

Furniture:

Wood is commonly used for crafting furniture, including chairs, tables, cabinets, and bed frames. Artisans may employ various woodworking techniques, such as carving, turning, and joinery, to create functional and aesthetically pleasing pieces.
